Frequently Asked Questions

Registration & Tickets

  • What is the difference between the Standard and Pro passes?

    • The Standard pass gives you full access to all three days of the conference, 48 technical
    talks, and 2 hands-on workshop sessions. The Pro pass upgrades your experience with
    unlimited workshop access, exclusive speaker meet-and-greets, a VIP networking dinner,
    lifetime recording access, and a 30-minute 1-on-1 mentorship session.

  • Can I upgrade my ticket later?

    • Yes. You can easily upgrade from a Standard pass to a Pro pass through
    your user dashboard at any time before the early-bird
    pricing window changes or until tickets sell out.

  • Do you offer group discounts for companies?

    • Yes! Our Team Pass is specifically bundled for up to 10 people
    at a flat rate of $5,999, which includes a dedicated lounge, private workshop booking, and
    recruitment perks. For teams larger than 10, click "Contact Us" in the
    pricing section for custom corporate rates.

  • What is your refund and ticket transfer policy?

    • Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event. If you cannot make it past the
    refund deadline, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague or friend at no additional cost.

Event Logistics & Content

  • Where is the conference being held?

    • DEVCONF 2026 is a hybrid event. The physical venue details can be found by clicking
    the Venue tab in the navigation bar. All keynotes, tracks, and workshops will also
    be streamed live for digital attendees.

  • How do I select and reserve my workshop sessions?

    • Once you register, you will receive an email invitation to build
    your custom schedule. Standard pass holders can choose their 2 preferred workshops, while
    Pro/Team pass holders can add unlimited sessions. Slots are first-come, first-served.

  • How long will I have access to the session recordings?

    • Standard ticket holders receive access to all digital recordings for 30 days
    after the event concludes. Pro and Team ticket holders receive permanent, lifetime access to the
    entire DEVCONF 2026 video archive
